Rikai x Aproach

To make sure your front-end development project is delivered on-time and without any hassles, we have crafted an all-encompassing framework comprised of six major phases:

Requirement Gathering and Understanding

Our Engineer will work face to face to identify all your requirements. We will make sure that the these are complete and there are no gaps between your expectations and what our developers will build.

Front-end Development Estimation

We will share and discuss with you our quotation and our time estimates.

Front-end Development Kick-Off

With the quotation and time estimation approved, we will introduce our team. No matter how many people are going to be involved in the actual development, we will provide you with a single point of contact (project manager) to ease the communication process.

Prototype

Our designers will submit the first draft for your approval. We will revise the concept based on your feedback to verify that the proposed solution meets all your unique requirements.

Delivery

Our project manager will make sure that the development of your front-end is always on track. He or she will proactively update you on our progress and will be happy to discuss any concerns you might have.

Maintenance

We knows that a front-end development project doesn’t stop when the final product is delivered. Our SE will work to keep your website up and running so you can focus on your business goals.
